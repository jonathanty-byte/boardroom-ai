# Script Video Demo — BoardRoom AI (90 secondes)

## Brief recommande pour la demo

**Question:** "Should we launch our SaaS product in the US market in Q1, or focus on consolidating our existing European base first?"

**CEO Vision:** "I want to move fast and capture the US before our competitors do."

Pourquoi ce brief:
- Tension strategique naturelle (croissance vs consolidation)
- CFO et CRO vont naturellement s'opposer
- CPO et CTO debattront de la readiness
- CEO vision cree une tension avec les membres prudents
- Garantit friction, debat, et probablement CEO follow-up

### SCENE 5 — Debats Multi-turn (0:34 - 0:55, 21s)

Scene principale. 2 echanges de debat, rapides, ping-pong.

**5a (0:34 - 0:44): Debat 1 — Piccolo vs Vegeta**

Overlay: `Debate 1 of 2: US Launch Risk`

Thread style terminal, messages avec effet de frappe rapide:

```
PICCOLO (CFO): Burn rate at current ARR makes Q1 US entry high-risk.
We'd need 18 months runway. We have 9.

VEGETA (CPO): Waiting 18 months means handing the market to
competitors. First-mover premium is real.

PICCOLO: First-mover means nothing without localization budget.
US enterprise sales cycles are 3x longer than EU.

VEGETA: Scale down initial scope — seed 3 cities, prove traction.
Low burn, high upside.
```

Status: `Turn 3 / 5  [CONVERGING]`

**5b (0:44 - 0:55): Debat 2 — Gohan vs Trunks**

Overlay: `Debate 2 of 2: Team Readiness`

```
GOHAN (CCO): CS team isn't scaled for US timezone coverage.
Churn risk in early accounts would damage the brand.

TRUNKS (CTO): Infrastructure is cloud-native, region switch is
2 sprints. Technical readiness is not the constraint.

GOHAN: Agreed on infra. Constraint is human capital — we need
2 CS hires minimum before US launch.

TRUNKS: That's a Q1 HR action, not a reason to delay. Hire
concurrent with ramp-up.
```

Status: `Turn 4 / 5  [IMPASSE]`

## Notes de Production

- **Pacing**: Ne jamais laisser tourner a la vitesse reelle du LLM. Pre-enregistrer puis accelerer x2-3 pendant le streaming.
- **Rythme visuel**: Cuts durs partout. Seule scene lente = Final Verdict (laisser respirer 5s).
- **Overlays**: Police monospace (theme RPG). Texte blanc sur fond sombre semi-transparent. 1 ligne max.
- **Musique**: Optionnelle. Lo-fi electronique, mid-tempo. Couper/baisser a la Scene 7.
- **Ne pas montrer**: Input API key, spinners, erreurs, settings, network tab. Happy path uniquement.
- **Resolution**: Enregistrer en 1440p, exporter en 1080p.
- **Outils suggeres**: OBS/Loom pour capture, DaVinci Resolve/CapCut pour overlays et speed ramps.
